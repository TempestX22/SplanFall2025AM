Shiitake — Lentinula edodes

The shiitake is a widely cultivated edible mushroom prized for its savory flavor and culinary versatility. Below are images and notes that experiment with proportion, crop and scale.

Ecology: Grows on hardwood stumps and logs; often cultivated on treated sawdust or logs.

Uses: Culinary ingredient with strong umami; source of lentinan for research in immunology.

Common names: Shiitake, fragrant mushroom.
