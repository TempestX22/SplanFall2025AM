Exploring fungal forms

Welcome to Mushroom Studies — a small catalogue where we examine form, function and flavor. This site experiments with typography, layout and information hierarchy.

A curated primer on a selection of familiar edible and notable species. Read through types, inspect images, and study one featured specimen.

Quick note: images are managed in images/ and all layout is controlled through styles.css. The work favors legibility, clear hierarchy and a neutral color palette.

Typography notes

This page uses two fonts: Libre Baskerville for headings and Almarai for body text. They are chosen for contrast: a classic serif for display headings, and a friendly sans for readable body text.

The word display is styled differently than the word annotation to show how spans can highlight meaning inline.

Sources: Mushroom Color Atlas — selected pages. Local images are stored in images/.
